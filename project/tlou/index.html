A Bilevel Framework for Optimal Price-Setting of Time-and-Level-of-Use Tariffs

Last updated on Mon, May 6, 2019

The pre-print of the presented work can be found on arXiv. The manuscript has also been submitted and should be published in the coming months.

This project was presented at several conferences, including:

  • Optimization days, Montréal, May 2018
  • NSERC Energy Storage Technology Network (NEST-Net) annual meeting, Toronto, June 2018. It was under the network’s project Optimal Brokerage Models for the Grid Integration of Energy Storage.
  • JuliaCon, London, August 2018 (poster)

An open-source Julia package has been released here for other researchers to model Time-and-Level-of-Use tariffs.
