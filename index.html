Biography

I’m a PhD candidate in a double program (cotutelle) between Ecole Polytechnique of Montréal, at the GERAD lab and the INRIA Lille, in the INOCS team, in mathematical optimization for smart grids. I especially focus on bilevel optimization problems and pricing for demand response. Other fields of interest include non-linear optimization & applications in engineering, integer optimization & decompositions, game theory and statistical learning. Before that I worked in various industries, from an IoT startup to steel manufacturing. I did my joint Bachelor-Master in Process Engineering at the UTC with a semester at the TUBS.

My academic CV is available upon request.
